Fun facts about Animals

Fun facts about Camels

  • Camels can go without water for 7 months
  • Camels live in Desert Hot Places/Locations
  • Camels are demostic animals

Fun facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads
  • Chameleons come in a lot of different shapes and sizes
  • Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues

Fun facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities

Fun facts about Bears

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  • Bears care deeply about family members

Fun facts about Pandas

  • Panda are a another species of Bears
  • Pandas love bamboo
  • A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear

Fun facts about Cicadas

  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus
  • Most have red-orange eyes
  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs
